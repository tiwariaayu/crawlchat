Released a [Chrome Extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/crawlchat/icimflpdiioobolkjdbldmmomflainie) for CrawlChat. You can use this tool to quickly compose text content from you knowledge base for different purposes and fill forms instantly on any web page. This would be very helpful for customer support teams to compose answers on support tickets, queries, DMs, emails etc. and for Product teams to fill forms for providing FAQs, product specifications, etc.

Read more about it [here](https://docs.crawlchat.app/chrome-extension)
